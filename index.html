Agent-Team — a verification workflow you can trust

Multi-agent orchestration · Evidence discipline

A three-role workflow that makes AI research checkable instead of merely fluent: a router turns a goal into a falsifiable brief, a research agent labels every claim by source and date, and an adversarial skeptic attacks the claims, math, and assumptions — and can demote any of them before a decision is made. The skeptic can weaken a claim but never strengthen one. Automated end-to-end with Claude Code.
